COMP 3104 is a course that teaches Development Operations using Github, Travis, Docker, etc.

This course is designed to better prepare professionals entering the IT field.
Specifically, a field where servers, web apps and infrastructure will be core to
business needs. COMP 3104 will provide a hands-on experience through the
implementation of tried, tested and true DevOps technologies. 

Students will have 10 Lab Exercises, 10 Quizzes, 1 individual and 1 group assignment, and finally midterm and final exam in this course.
For Lab Exercises and Quizzes, only 8 quizzes out of 10 will be considered as final mark.
